Our Design Workflow

A structured approach to engineering design and documentation.

1

Initial Consultation

Detailed discussion to understand project requirements, constraints, and objectives to establish design parameters.

2

Concept Development

Creation of initial design concepts and sketches to explore potential solutions and approaches.

3

3D Modeling

Precision 3D modeling of components and assemblies in Autodesk Inventor for visualization and validation.

4

Design Review

Collaborative review of designs to ensure they meet specifications and functional requirements.

5

Documentation

Creation of comprehensive technical drawings and documentation for manufacturing and assembly.

6

Final Delivery

Delivery of complete engineering package including 3D models, technical drawings, and documentation.
